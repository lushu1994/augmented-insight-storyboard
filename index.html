01HomePrimary▶
Hero — full screen
Cinematic drone footage or high-res building photo. Headline: "See Every Inch. Plan With Confidence." Subhead roots the company in NYC facade inspection and photogrammetry. One CTA: "View Our Work."
Value proposition strip
4 columns — Comprehensive Coverage / Hard-to-Reach Access / Structured Deliverables / FISP & Compliance Support. Short phrases only.

6 service tiles with thumbnail, title, and one-line description. Each links to its dedicated service page.

3 cards side by side: New York Stock Exchange, The Pierre, The Plaza Hotel. Each card shows a strong image, building name, and one line describing what was done. Links to individual project pages. No slider — all three visible at once.
Who we serve
Audience identifiers: Architects / Engineers / Facade Consultants / Building Owners / Property Managers / Contractors. Brief value statement per type.
FAQ snippet block
3–4 structured Q&As targeting AI-search queries: "What is drone facade inspection?" / "How does photogrammetry support FISP compliance?" Structured for AEO — helps AI tools like ChatGPT and Perplexity cite Augmented Insight as a source.
Blog preview — 2 latest posts from Insights
Internal link into the blog. Reduces bounce and reinforces topical authority.
Social media feed strip
Live or curated feed pulling recent Instagram and LinkedIn posts. Keeps the site feeling active and current. Links to social profiles.
Closing CTA band
"Get a clearer view of your building." → Contact page.
SEO / AEO
Primary targets: "drone facade inspection NYC," "FISP drone inspection," "building photogrammetry New York." FAQ block structured for Google AI Overview and ChatGPT citations.

08Blog — "Insights"Primary▶
Page header
Titled "Insights" — industry knowledge, technical context, and building documentation best practices. The blog lives under this name across the entire site.
Post grid
Featured post (large). 3-column card grid for additional posts. Category tags: FISP / Photogrammetry / NYC Buildings / Technology / Industry.
Linked post series
Posts are organized into related groups — for example, several posts all covering different angles of FISP compliance, each linking to one another. This keeps readers on the site longer and signals to search engines that Augmented Insight is a credible authority on these topics.
Social media distribution
Each Insights post is repurposed and distributed across LinkedIn and Instagram — one piece of content, multiple touchpoints.
SEO / AEO
Insights posts target questions that architects, engineers, and building owners are already searching for — topics like FISP deadlines, facade inspection methods, and drone access in NYC. Well-structured posts on these topics get picked up and cited by AI tools like ChatGPT and Perplexity, putting Augmented Insight in front of people before they even reach a search results page.
Recommended publishing cadence
2 posts per week. Enough to build consistent momentum and signal activity to search engines, without sacrificing the quality and technical depth that this audience expects. At this pace, the Insights section becomes a meaningful content library within 6 months.

03 — Internal linking strategy
Every service page → related projects + contact
Each service page links to 2+ projects that used it, plus a contact CTA. Creates loops that reduce bounce and move users toward conversion.
Every project page → service tags + related projects
Service chips link back to service pages. Prev / Next navigation between projects. No page ends in a dead end.
Insights posts → services + projects
Every post links to at least one service page and one project. Keeps readers on the site and drives them toward conversion pages.
Home → everywhere
Homepage links to all primary pages. It is the hub. All pages connect back through nav and breadcrumbs. No page more than 2 clicks from any other.
04 — SEO & AEO strategy
Traditional SEO
Search engine optimization
Keyword-targeted pages for each service and project. Long-tail terms like "drone facade inspection NYC" and "FISP drone photogrammetry" are low-competition, high-intent. Linked post series built through Insights drive authority over time.
AEO — Answer Engine Optimization
Optimizing for AI tools
AEO is how content gets cited by ChatGPT, Perplexity, and Google AI Overviews. Requires structured FAQ blocks, clear authoritative answers, and well-organized content around topics the target audience is already asking about.
Content priorities
What to build first
At launch: core pages + 4–6 Insights posts. Priority topics: FISP compliance + drones / photogrammetry in building inspection / how drone inspections reduce access risk. Each post answers a real question an architect or engineer would search for.
